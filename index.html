  • Writing Center

    The Writing Center provides free, friendly help with any of your writing assignments—at any stage of your writing process.

    Peer tutors will work with you in a 45-minute, one-on-one appointment to clarify your argument, organize your ideas, correctly document your research, or polish your grammar.

  • Math Lab

    Math Lab provides a helpful environment for anyone in need of help on thier Math class assignments.

    Tutors can help students through problems, check their answers, or provide study tips for upcoming tests.

    •

  • Computer Science Lab

    Come to CS Lab for help on Intro to Programming, Scientific Computing, or Computer Science 1 coding assigments.

    Tutors are available to help students through coding assignments or provide study tips for upcoming tests.

    •

  • Business Lab

    Business Lab help sessions provide direct access to business and economics tutors on a drop-in basis.

    Tutors can review assignments with you, clarify course concepts and suggest effective study strategies.

    •

  • Science Lab

    Science Lab offers help sessions for Biology and Chemistry classes.

    Tutors are available to help students with homework or studying for future tests.
