Face to Face Reservations
Reserve or cancel a spot for a face-to-face session


Reservations:

To attend in a face-to-face format, students must make a reservation. This process helps us maintain Covid capacities for rooms. Reservations can be made 3 days prior to the session start, and until the session ends.

Cancellation:

If you need to cancel a reservation, you may also use the “Face to Face Reservations.”

Attending a Face-to-Face Session:

  • Check your email before a session to assure there have not been any last minute cancellations.
  • Bring your ID to the session when you attend, as your reservation will be checked.
  • Please try to arrive at the start of the session, as it's easier to work through problems together as a group. However, you may arrive at any time during the session, as long as there is space.
Virtual Sign On
Join a virtual help session


Make sure you have set up your Bethel Zoom account, and it’s helpful to download the desktop client here.

  • When you click “Virtual Sign On,” all currently-open sessions for your courses will be listed.
  • Select the session you wish to attend (so your attendance can be recorded) and you will be given a single-use Zoom link to join the session.
  • Remember to come back to end your attendance.
